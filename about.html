Our Story

Shany's Handmade Toys is a partnership business that was established in 2022. It was founded by Makanaka Mhuruyengwe and Tariro Mapuwei.

Our company's objective is to create toys for every child that resonate with their culture, history, and personal stories.

This coincides with our slogan: A Toy for Every Child. A Memory for Every Heart.

Founder Quote

"Sometimes all a child needs is to see themselves reflected in something they love and that's where everything begins."
~ Shania Mhuruyengwe

We are committed to being actively involved in our communities while creating handmade, customizable toys that reflect the diversity of the nation we live in.

Our toys are designed for every child in South Africa, crafted by skilled artisans who pour care, creativity, and heart into every piece.

Our vision is to build a safe, inclusive, and joyful space where every child feels seen, valued, and represented. We aim to celebrate individuality through toys that embrace different cultures, abilities, and backgrounds, while promoting creativity, learning, and happiness.

We believe in creating more than just toys. We create meaningful experiences, lasting memories, and a sense of belonging for every child.

We are guided by inclusivity, diversity, integrity, safety, and quality in everything we do.

We are dedicated to operating responsibly and ethically when creating our toys.
From ensuring fair and respectful treatment of our employees to using sustainable
materials and environmentally friendly packaging, we strive to make a positive impact
on both people and the planet. Through our community initiatives, we support orphanages,
Early Childhood Development centres, and local partnerships that help uplift children around us.

FAQ

Do you offer customizable toys?
Yes, we offer customizable toys where a child can add additional features so that the toy accurately represents them.

What materials are your toys made from?
Our toys are made from high-quality, child-safe materials including wood, 100% cotton, cotton blends, and linen.

Are your toys safe for all ages?
Yes, all our toys meet safety standards. We also provide age recommendations to ensure the safest experience.

Do you deliver across South Africa?
No, we do not deliver nationwide. We have a physical store in Mafikeng. However, we do offer a third-party courier at an extra cost.

Can I return or exchange a toy?
Yes, we have a return and exchange policy. Please contact us for assistance.
